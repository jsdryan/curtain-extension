# Chrome Web Store Listing

## Extension Name
Curtain - Privacy Curtain

## Short Description (max 132 chars)
Hide sensitive bookmarks & browsing history with one click. Restore them when you're ready. 100% local, zero data collection.

## Detailed Description (English)

Curtain lets you instantly hide sensitive browsing history and bookmarks before sharing your screen — and restore everything when you're done.

**How it works:**
- Click "Pull Down Curtain" to hide matching history and bookmarks
- Click "Restore All" to bring everything back
- All data stays on your device. Nothing is ever sent anywhere.

**Features:**
- One-click hide and restore for browsing history and bookmarks
- Keyword matching catches related content on any site (e.g., related tools on GitHub, discussions on Reddit)
- Fully customizable site and keyword lists
- Temporarily disables Chrome search suggestions to prevent address bar leaks
- Visual indicator shows when Curtain is active

**Permissions explained:**
- Bookmarks: Read and restore your bookmarks
- History: Read and restore your browsing history
- Storage: Save your settings and backup data locally
- Privacy: Temporarily disable search suggestions while active

**Perfect for:**
- Screen sharing in meetings
- Live demos and presentations
- When someone borrows your laptop
- Any time your browser might be seen by others

All processing happens locally on your device. Curtain never collects, transmits, or shares any data. See our privacy policy for details.

## Detailed Description (Traditional Chinese / 繁體中文)

Curtain 讓你在分享螢幕前，一鍵隱藏敏感的瀏覽紀錄和書籤，結束後再一鍵恢復。

**使用方式：**
- 點擊「拉下簾幕」隱藏匹配的紀錄與書籤
- 點擊「恢復全部」還原所有內容
- 所有資料都留在你的裝置上，絕不外傳

**功能特色：**
- 一鍵隱藏與恢復瀏覽紀錄及書籤
- 關鍵字比對功能，可捕捉任何網站上的相關內容（例如 GitHub 上的相關工具、Reddit 上的討論）
- 完全自訂網站和關鍵字清單
- 簾幕啟用時自動關閉 Chrome 搜尋建議，防止網址列洩露
- 視覺指示器顯示簾幕狀態

**權限說明：**
- 書籤：讀取及還原你的書籤
- 瀏覽紀錄：讀取及還原你的瀏覽紀錄
- 儲存空間：在本地儲存設定和備份資料
- 隱私：在簾幕啟用期間暫時關閉搜尋建議

**適用場景：**
- 會議投影分享螢幕
- 作品展示與 Demo
- 借電腦給他人使用
- 任何瀏覽器可能被他人看到的場合

所有處理都在你的裝置上完成。Curtain 絕不收集、傳輸或分享任何資料。詳見隱私政策。
